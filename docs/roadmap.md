# Roadmap

## MVP

Target: single-branch pharmacy or drug store with 3 to 20 staff.

Includes:

- desktop app with offline mode
- client records and notes
- product catalog and stock movements
- appointment calendar
- invoicing and payment collection
- SMS reminders and receipt notifications
- core reports for revenue, low stock, and daily activity
- admin, cashier, pharmacist, and manager roles
- cloud backup and device sync for one branch

## Phase 2

- multi-branch inventory transfer
- bank reconciliation
- document vault with OCR-ready ingestion
- Arabic UI pack
- mobile companion for approvals and notifications
- advanced analytics with cohort and retention views
- recurring billing and subscription services

## Full platform

- marketplace integrations for payments and logistics
- procurement workflows and supplier management
- insurance and institutional billing
- AI-assisted insights and multilingual message suggestions
- workflow automation builder
- microservice extraction for notifications, analytics, and sync
- regional SaaS control plane with tenant provisioning
