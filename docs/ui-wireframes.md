# UI Wireframes

## 1. Desktop dashboard

Layout:

- left rail with modules, sync pulse, branch selector, and quick create
- top bar with global search, date range, theme toggle, language toggle, and profile
- primary canvas split into command header, KPIs, live schedule, revenue view, and operational alerts
- right insight column for sync health, low stock, payment mix, and recent messages

Key actions:

- create sale
- book appointment
- receive stock
- send SMS reminder
- export report

## 2. CRM workspace

Layout:

- searchable client list on the left
- profile summary in the middle
- interaction timeline and documents on the right

## 3. Calendar and scheduling

Layout:

- staff availability strip across the top
- day/week/month toggle
- calendar grid as primary surface
- appointment drawer for edit, confirmation, reminders, and billing link

## 4. Billing and checkout

Layout:

- client context and prescription or service context on the left
- bill builder in the center
- totals, payment methods, and issue receipt panel on the right

## 5. Inventory control

Layout:

- product search and filters
- stock table with quantity, batch, expiry, and reorder state
- movement panel showing receipts, adjustments, returns, and transfers

## 6. Messaging hub

Layout:

- thread list left
- conversation center
- automation templates and delivery status right

## 7. Reports and analytics

Layout:

- filter bar at top
- large revenue chart
- payment mix, appointment utilization, top-selling medicines, and low-stock forecast below
- export drawer for PDF and spreadsheet

## 8. Onboarding

Flow:

1. business profile
2. branch and currency setup
3. staff and roles
4. services and product import
5. payment methods
6. SMS provider setup
7. offline sync test
